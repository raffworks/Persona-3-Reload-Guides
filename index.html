PERSONA 3 RELOAD GUIDES

Original guide by Monomaru on powerpyx.com

This walkthrough is designed to help you achieve a full completion run in Persona 3 Reload. It covers all key progression elements including Social Links, Linked Episodes, Dorm interactions, trophies, and optional computer-related activities. You can also follow it to manage romance routes and plan shrine visits with different characters. Romance choices are highlighted in Pink, and completing at least one romance route is required to unlock the That Special Someone achievement.

Some Dorm activities, especially those involving characters like Fuuka and Yukari, are important for strengthening Social Links and contributing toward specific achievements such as Dorm Life, Eat Your Veggies, Peas!, and Through Thick and Thin.

Linked Episodes are optional for completion purposes, but they are still included in this guide as they provide additional story content and unlock unique Personas that can be used for fusion.

Computer-related tasks are only relevant once your Social Stats are fully developed. However, certain story days (such as 12/10 and 12/11) require specific choices like the Dating Site Note.

If you miss matching Arcana requirements on certain days, your Social Link progression may fall behind. This can usually be corrected on flexible days with optional activities. However, skipping Linked Episode days may result in missing some exclusive Persona fusion opportunities.
